# Financial Performance Dashboard

## Overview
This project is part of my Data Analyst Internship at Karmix Tech. It analyzes 
regional and category-level business performance using the Sample Superstore dataset.

## Business Problem
Identify which regions and product categories are driving profitability, and 
where the business may be losing money.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX

## Key Insights
- West and East regions are the strongest profit performers, while Central lags behind
- Technology is the top revenue-generating category
- Consumer segment contributes the most to overall profit
- Central region's Furniture line is operating at a loss

## Recommendation
Review discount and cost structures for Furniture sales in the Central region, 
and apply pricing strategies from West/East regions to underperforming regions.

## Dataset
Sample Superstore Dataset (Kaggle, CC0 Public Domain)
